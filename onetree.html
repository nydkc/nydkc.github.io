One Tree Planted



One Tree Planted is a 501(c)(3) non-profit founded in Shelburne, Vermont focused on global reforestation. One dollar. One tree.

watch_later

Story

Started in 2014 with a mission to make it simple for people to help the environment.

language

Where?

We plant trees in North America, South America, Asia, and Africa. Most of our projects help with reforestation from forest fires and floods, as well as creating jobs, building communities, and protecting habitat for biodiversity.

nature_people

Why We Love Trees

Planting trees is such a simple and effective way to make an impact. Trees clean the air we breathe, the water we drink, and they help stabalize our climate.
